OUR STORY

Protecting animals is protecting our future

Founded in 2018, WildGuard has grown into one of the world's most trusted wildlife conservation organizations. We work directly with local communities, governments, and scientists to stop poaching, restore ecosystems, and ensure animals thrive in the wild.
